About

The iconic institute of Sri Venkateswara College, University of Delhi has ever since been tirelessly working to fulfill its mission of imparting education. To mark the commencement of the 60th year of Sri Venkateswara College, the Department of Political Science is organizing Vritतान्त (#VocalisingVisions) a 3-day Virtual Conclave as a part of the college's Diamond Jubilee celebrations.
